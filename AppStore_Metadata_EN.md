## Description (4000 characters max)
```
◆ Reduce Food Waste. Save Money.

The average household throws away hundreds of dollars worth of food each year. FreshKeeper makes it easy to track expiry dates by simply pointing your camera — helping you waste less food and save more money.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
◆ KEY FEATURES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

【📷 Instant Camera Scanning】
• Scan barcodes to auto-fill product names
• OCR reads expiry dates from food labels automatically
• Supports multiple date formats (YYYY/MM/DD, YYYY.MM.DD, and more)
• Register items in seconds — no manual typing needed

【🏠 Smart Food List】
• Color-coded by urgency: red (expiring), orange (soon), green (safe)
• Filter by storage: refrigerator, freezer, or pantry
• Swipe left to mark as consumed, right to mark as discarded

【🔔 Expiry Notifications】
• Choose to be notified 1, 3, or 7 days before expiry
• Customize notification time (6:00 AM – 10:00 PM)
• Never let food expire unnoticed again

【📊 Savings Dashboard】
• See your monthly savings at a glance
• Weekly bar charts show your progress
• Track consumption rate and lifetime savings
• Compare month-to-month improvement

【📱 Home & Lock Screen Widgets】
• Check expiring items without opening the app
• Small and medium widget sizes
• Lock screen badge for quick updates

【☁️ iCloud Sync】
• Seamlessly sync across all your Apple devices
• Keep your data when switching phones
• Toggle on/off anytime

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
◆ PRIVACY FIRST
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• All camera processing happens on your device
• No data sent to external servers
• No ads, no tracking, no analytics
• Your food data stays yours — always

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
◆ PERFECT FOR
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• Anyone tired of finding expired food in the fridge
• Budget-conscious shoppers looking to cut food costs
• People who care about reducing food waste and sustainability
• Emergency supply and pantry stock management
• Busy individuals who need a quick, effortless solution

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Completely free. No in-app purchases. No subscriptions.
Start your zero food waste journey today.
```
